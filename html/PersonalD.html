what you love, you’re making an excuse.” I try to Feed myself well, Move my Body,
Practice Self-compassion, Meditate, Avoid energy drains, spend time with enthusiastic people and
Learn to say NO.

As a Determined Person, I keep myself motivated and stick to my plan, I learn
how to handle my stress, I try to find out my 'why', Value my health, Create postitive
habits and always Practicing.

As a Dedicated Person I try not to argue with the plan, know that every day matters,
I try to Build a No-matter-what mindset Plan a Routine, Try to Commit and Find pleasure
in the hard work.
